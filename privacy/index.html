Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Best Findings USA does not directly collect any personal information from viewers of our content. We do not operate user accounts, login systems, or data collection forms. Our content is published exclusively through third-party platforms which have their own data practices.

2. Platform Analytics

We use analytics provided by the platforms we publish on (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) to understand content performance. This data includes:

  • Aggregate view counts, likes, comments, and shares
  • General audience demographics (age ranges, geographic regions)
  • Content performance metrics over time

This data is aggregated and anonymized by the platform — we cannot and do not identify individual users through these analytics.

3. TikTok API Usage

We use TikTok's Content Posting API and Video List API to publish and manage content on our own TikTok accounts. We only access data for accounts we own and have explicitly authorized. We do not access, collect, or store data from other TikTok users' accounts.

4. Affiliate Links & Cookies

When you click an affiliate link in our content, you are redirected to a third-party retailer (such as Amazon). That retailer may use cookies or similar technologies to track the referral and your subsequent browsing or purchase activity on their site. This tracking is governed entirely by the retailer's privacy policy, not ours. We receive only aggregated commission reports — we do not receive your personal information from retailers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise transfer any personal data because we do not collect personal data. We have no user database, mailing list, or customer records.

6. Children's Privacy

Our content is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has somehow provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our content exists on platforms with their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of:

  • TikTok — tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Amazon — amazon.com/privacy
  • Instagram — instagram.com/privacy
  • YouTube — policies.google.com/privacy

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact

For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bestfindingsusa@gmail.com
